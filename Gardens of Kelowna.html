Gardens of Kelowna

Welcome to the Flower Gardens of the Okanagan

As the Okanagan is widely known for its agriculture, some advancements in horticulture and gardening can be found here. The following links below are privately owned or public flower gardens in of the Okanagan:

The Okanagan Lavender and Herb Farm

Pioneer Gardens

On Kelowna's Waterfront Park, the Pioneer Gardens that are immaculately kept for everyones to see as you pass near the iconic Dolphin Fountains across from the Propera Place event centre.

Knowles Heritage Park

Veendam Gardens

The Veedam Gardens are in Kelowna's City Park, where important public events such as Remembrance Day, are held each year.

Guishigan Heritage Park

The Guishigan Heritage Park is a gorgeous historical venue that is suitable for picnic lunches, family or corporate events and weddings.

Kasugai Gardens

In the heart of downtown Kelowna are the traditionally landscaped sunken Kasugai Japanese Gardens. It has great walking paths and a tranquil atmosphere.

Oasis Lavender Farm, North of Summerland in the Garnet Valley
